Menu Engineering Principles

Our AI applies these proven scientific techniques to your menu

Matrix Classification

Uses the Star-Plow-Puzzle-Dog matrix to classify items based on popularity and profitability, allowing for strategic decisions for each category.

Golden Triangle

Places high-profit items in the visual sweet spot where eyes naturally go first, increasing the likelihood they'll be ordered.

Price Anchoring

Strategic price arrangement to influence value perception and guide customers toward high-margin items.

Visual Hierarchy

Uses design elements like boxes, color, font variation and spacing to draw attention to high-profit items.

Decoy Effect

Strategically positions certain items to make others seem more attractive, leveraging cognitive biases.

Choice Architecture

Optimizes menu length and section organization to prevent choice overload while maximizing profitability.
